ABOUT ME

As an award-winning Executive Creative Director at The Marketing Arm, I've spent 20 years building and leading a team of 150+ creatives alongside a roster of talented freelancers.

My leadership style is rooted in servant leadership — working alongside my team, recognising each person's strengths, and building an environment where every voice contributes to exceptional work.

I've worked across graphic design, illustration, experiential, POS, and virtual reality — for clients including PepsiCo, Disney, Pernod Ricard, Philips and Wendy's.

Not here for the trophy. Here for the work. I just want to make things that feel like something — with a team that lifts each other up, laughs hard, and goes home proud of what they built together.

My ethos: do great work, then make it even better.
